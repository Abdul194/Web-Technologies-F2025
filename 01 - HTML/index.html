Course Objective

Web Technologies is a dynamic field that explores the principles and methodologies driving the development and optimization of web-based applications, enabling efficient communication and interaction over the internet.

Throughout this course, students will engage with case studies and real-world examples, gaining insight into the practical implementation of web technologies across various applications. Through hands-on exercises, they will develop proficiency in building robust and scalable web applications that meet modern industry standards and user expectations.
